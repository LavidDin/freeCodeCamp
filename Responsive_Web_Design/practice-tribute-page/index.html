Ulrich Alexander "Rick" Fox (born July 24, 1969) is a Canadian-Bahamian[1] film and television actor, businessman, retired basketball player, and esports franchise owner. He played in the National Basketball Association (NBA) for the Boston Celtics and Los Angeles Lakers, and played college basketball for the North Carolina Tar Heels.
